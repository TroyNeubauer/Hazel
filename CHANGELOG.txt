Hazel is a high performanace cross platform C++ game engine that supports the folowing platforms:
 *Windows
 *Linux
 *MacOSX (Needs devolepment)
 *Android (not started)
 *IOS (not started)


For building installing on linux, the:
 * xorg-dev
 *e2fslibs-dev
 *nettle-dev
 *acl-dev
 *libghc-bzlib-dev
 *lzma-dev
 *lz4

packages must be installed.
